    Welcome... We're Glad You Found Us!

    The Columbia Memorial Space Center is a hands-on learning center dedicated to bringing the wonder and excitement of science and innovation to audiences of all ages and backgrounds. Through world-class programs and engaging exhibits, the Space Center strives to ignite a community of critical and creative thinkers.

    Features and Services

    The Columbia Memorial Space Center is proud to offer a wide variety of educational programs such as field trips, scout programs and other organized group events e.g. birthday parties and private or corporate events.
